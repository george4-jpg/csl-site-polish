---
name: George4 Proprietary Positioning Rules
description: Copy must create intrigue without revealing proprietary methods, frameworks, or implementation details
type: constraint
---
Do not reveal proprietary methods, internal frameworks, specific orchestration logic, agent structures, validation techniques, or strategic implementation details on any George4 or AI Leadership content.

Use hooks, signals, and high-level positioning only. Describe the category of capability, not the underlying mechanics.

Allowed phrasing:
- "strategic AI architecture" (not how it is architected)
- "multi-model orchestration" (not how routing decisions are made)
- "governance for intelligent systems" (not the specific governance model)
- "checks, balances, and human oversight" (not the internal control design)
- "practical operating systems for AI leadership" (not the full operating framework)

The copy should make serious leaders curious enough to engage, not informed enough to replicate the approach.
